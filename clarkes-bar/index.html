Our Story

A real Irish pub,
right here in Alicante.

Clarke's Bar was founded in 2006 as a family-run Irish pub in the heart of Playa Flamenca. What started as a neighbourhood watering hole has grown into the beating social heart of the community — a place where regulars become friends, where every pour is made with care, and where the craic is always good.

Whether you're here for a perfectly kept pint, a glass of Rioja on the terrace, or a quiet Tuesday that somehow turns into a Thursday — you're always welcome at Clarke's.
